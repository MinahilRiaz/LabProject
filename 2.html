ABOUT US

OUR JOURNEY:

We started our dream at a very small stage by working from home and deivering our delicacies throughout the city. As our business grew, we thought of opening up our branch. We started from a small building with takeout option only. Soon, we expanded our restaurant to a full-hall dine-in restaurant. And now here we are, serving you with the best of cuisine in your very own city. You can definitely expect to see us in other cities as well as we are expanding our business countrywide seeing the wholesome response we received from our city.
